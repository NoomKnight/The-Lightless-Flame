Seat of the Party

The Duchy of Greenwatch

Everything Greenwatch in one place. The keep, the people, the army, and the house beneath the house.

The Keep

Granted by King Aleric Valethorn the 5th after the Titus business. It came with two green dragons; it doesn’t have them anymore. Terrick’s crews have been building ever since, and with the royal writ, six hundred settlers and a team of royal specialists are on their way. The duchy is about to double.

The People

  • Steve (Stephen Rogers) — runs the garrison and drills the troops. Most of them are kobolds, and he’s making real soldiers out of them.
  • Christopher Hasenpfeffer — William’s brother. Fourteen. Runs the library.
  • Shadow — Strixhaven-trained rogue. Found at the Allfield academy and brought to Strixhaven by the party. Now lives at Greenwatch.
  • Melethor — Shadow’s friend from Strixhaven.
  • Sterling Pemberton — Strixhaven history professor. Came out with the party and never really left.
  • Brock — an amethyst dragon. Lives at the keep. On our side.
  • Despona — drow doctor and informant. Provided the map of the deep roads. Lives at Greenwatch under Steve’s watch.
  • Terrick — master artisan. Builds faster than the duchy can plan.
  • Mira — painter. Working on the mural.
  • Phelban Shelkesha (“Blue Corn”) — duergar informant under the duchy’s protection.
  • Iroh — with the party.
  • Mila & Thor — a blink dog and a good boy.

The Army

Minnesota: the First Minnesota Volunteer Regiment, recruited from the overlooked across the whole continent, starting with the kobolds. (Minnesota is also the name of the heaven William described to them. They are working toward it.)

  • Boldwin the Bold — the kobolds’ leader.
  • Rho Khetshypp — lieutenant. Red dragonborn out of Ashbourne.
  • Aero Planes — lieutenant and archer, out of Allfield. Hawk named Jett.
  • The Freeblade Commission — mercenaries on retainer. Fifty gold a month and a house after a year. Mai Dovekin is stationed here with the detachment.

The House Beneath the House

Under the dining room is a buried dwarven complex nobody knew about. In it: the chained ghosts of Marley and Marley, a bottomless pit bound in chains, an unfinished instrument missing its water parts, and a living root of the World Tree sitting on a node of the ley lines.

The root is also a gate. Opened once, with the crystals. It showed the Seventh Circle. It is closed.

William swore an oath to the tree: protect the node, let no one harm it, and never open a portal, for as long as he can. The tree later told Erik the root is his responsibility now. Erik keeps the crystals and follows the care instructions.

What the walls say:

“Here labored the crew of Master Delruk, Fifth Keeper of the Root.”
“The keeper’s touch must be alive.”
“No forgefire without a song. No song without breath.”
“Seventh conduit sealed. Four remain untested.”

Holdings & Friends of the Duchy

  • Cattlekeep — held by Theodric and Nyssa Greylark in the party’s name.
  • The Emberhold — the dwarven hold and the party’s allies. Thrain is High Mason. Chanticlear was theirs. The Emberhold dwarves work the nearby Stonebriar mines, where the tremors first led and the machine was found.
